We are Colorado's Largest Premier FPV Racing Club, we are also a Tier 1 Chapter of MultiGP, in which we promote safe and fun FPV racing and we are also sponsored by some of the largest companies in the Multirotor and FPV industries. This group is also here for the beginners as well as the advanced pilots to get together and learn more about racing, FPV, and anything related to this hobby.

Join our Facebook group where you can post builds, see what others are building, learn about electronics, discuss tuning, and anything else related to flying or racing multirotors. Or go straight to our page on MultiGP.com and sign up for a race!
